Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload an image?

Click the "Upload Image" button on the home page to select an image from your device.

How can I remove the background?

After uploading, the background will be automatically removed on the Result page. Use the editing tools if needed.

What is Custom Background?

You can add or select custom backgrounds on the Custom Background page to apply behind your image.
